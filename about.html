Biography


Jordi Tolosa born one night in October 1956.
He studied at the Sant Jordi School of Fine Art, in Barcelona.
During the 80s began a series of interventions in the street, both individually and collectively.
He have study and residence in Barcelona.
After a short stay in Germany, shows in Hannover, Bonn and Frankfurt.
His work has been exhibited in museums and galleries in Spain, France and Germany.
